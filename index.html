About Sama

Sama Asheh is a Palestinian artist with a love for capturing people's stories through art. I started drawing strangers in public places, gifting them their portraits without expecting anything in return. For me, art is a bridge no words, just a pencil and a litte kindness. My work is inspired by real moments, human connections, and the beauty of everyday life. Now based in Atlanta, I continue to create portraits, murals, and custom artwork, sharing pieces of my heart with every stroke.
